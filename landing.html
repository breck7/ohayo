ohayo!

ohayo is a research prototype for a new family of programming languages called Tree Languages. Tree Languages are like Lisps without parentheses.

Why use Tree Languages over Lisps? Tree Languages use a very simple geometric syntax that we think will make programs easier to develop, analyze and maintain at scale.
